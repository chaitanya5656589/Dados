Minor Districts of Gandaki Province

These districts have lower smartphone user counts but are rich in natural beauty, cultural heritage, and unique geographical features.

Lamjung

A district with rich biodiversity and the starting point of the Annapurna Circuit trek. Has 95,000 smartphone users.

Parbat

A small but culturally rich district with significant religious sites. Has 85,000 smartphone users.

Myagdi

Known for its diverse landscapes and as the gateway to the Dhaulagiri mountain range. Has 65,000 smartphone users.

Mustang

Famous for its ancient kingdom of Lo and desert-like landscape in the rain shadow of the Himalayas. Has only 7,500 smartphone users despite its tourism importance.

Manang

A high-altitude district in the trans-Himalayan region, known for its unique culture and trekking routes. Has the lowest smartphone user count at 2,800.
