Major Districts of Gandaki Province

These are the major districts of Gandaki Province based on smartphone users, population, and infrastructure.

Kaski

Home to Pokhara, the provincial capital and a major tourist destination. Kaski is the economic and administrative hub of Gandaki Province with the highest smartphone user count (310,000).

Tanahun

A major district with significant agricultural production and the Seti River hydropower projects. Has 180,000 smartphone users.

Nawalpur

The newest district in Gandaki Province, formed after the administrative restructuring of Nepal. It has 175,000 smartphone users and a diverse geography ranging from plains to hills.

Syangja

Known for its agricultural production and as a major supplier of vegetables to urban centers. Has 165,000 smartphone users.

Baglung

A major district with rich cultural heritage and developing infrastructure. Has 150,000 smartphone users.

Gorkha

Historically significant district, known for being the origin of the Shah dynasty that unified Nepal. Has 145,000 smartphone users.
